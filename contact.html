Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need prior dance experience to join?

A: Not at all! We welcome dancers of all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced performers. Our training sessions are designed to accommodate everyone.

Q: What should I bring to practice sessions?

A: Just wear comfortable clothes that allow free movement, bring a water bottle, and most importantly, bring your enthusiasm! We have sound systems and mirrors in our practice room.

Q: Are there any membership fees?

A: We have a nominal annual membership fee that covers basic club activities. Additional costs may apply for special workshops, costumes, or competition participation.

Q: Can I learn multiple dance styles?

A: Absolutely! We encourage our members to explore different dance styles. Our weekly sessions cover various forms, and you're welcome to attend any or all of them.

Q: How can I stay updated with club activities?

A: Follow us on our social media channels and join our WhatsApp group for regular updates about practices, events, and special announcements.
